The BEST Fajita Recipe Of This Century

Description

Make some DELICIOUS beef fajitas with the following recipe. This will be the envy of any man that has attempted to grill a piece of meat. This formula was inspired by Montezuma himself and passed down through many generations only to be lost in the battle of el Alamo. After many attempts at recreating, I FINALLY perfected the recipe. The Aztec gods would be proud. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of outside skirt steak
  • 1 bundle of cilantro
  • 4 cups of olive oil
  • 4 TBSP of El Rancho Red Fajita Seasoning (click link to order)
  • 2 limes

Steps

  1. Take the skirt steak and with a sharp knife remove any excess fat around the meat
  2. Take the cilantro bundle and twist off the bottom half of the bundle discarding the stems and keeping just the leaves
  3. Roughly chop the cilantro leaves
  4. In a large bowl pour the 4 cups of olive oil
  5. Dip the skirt steak in the olive oil, let the excess oil drip into the bowl, and then move the skirt steak to the cutting board
