Salsa Verde Recipe

Description

Today marks the day that will make you a legend in your family. Many have attempted to make a green mixture that they call "salsa" BUT have failed. You my friend, will succeed and your legacy will live on. The following recipe will change your life forever. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 18 green tomatillos
  • 5 jalapeños
  • 1 poblano pepper
  • 1 brown onion
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 bundle of cilantro
  • 1 green pepper
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp of peppercorn
  • 1 tsp of coriander seeds
  • 1 TBSP of chicken bouillon
  • 2 TBSP of salt
  • 4 cups of water

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan add all the ingredients except for the salt, cilantro and 3 garlic cloves
  2. Boil ingredients for 8-12 mins or until tomatillos and onions change color
  3. Turn off heat and remove the tomatillos, onion, 3 jalapeños, poblano pepper and add them to a blender
  4. Remove 1 cup of liquid from the sauce pan and add to blender
  5. Add fresh ingredients to blender: cilantro, 3 garlic cloves, 1 1/2 TBSP of salt
  6. Blend all the ingredients for 1 minute or until fully blended
  7. Taste salsa for spice and salt level
  8. Add a jalapeños or salt, blend and taste
  9. Repeat step 8 until desired taste and spice is achieved
