Tacos Aztecas Recipe

Description

This recipe combines the Montezuma Skirt Steak with a few more ingredients and tops it with a salsa verde to make the most mouthwatering tacos anyone has ever had!

Ingredients

  • Montezuma Skirt Steak
  • 1 bag of Guerrero corn tortillas (link)
  • 1 TBSP of olive oil
  • 1 brown onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 orange pepper
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 1 lime
  • 2 TBSP of salt
  • 1/2 TBSP of pepper
  • 1/2 tsp of onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp of garlic powder
  • 1 TBSP of salsa verde

Steps

  1. Cut onion and peppers in long strips
  2. Dice garlic cloves, cut lime in half
  3. Put a fry pan over med-high heat for 3 mins
  4. Add olive oil to fry pan and let it sit for 30 seconds
  5. Add onion, 1/2 TBSP of salt and cook for 3 mins
  6. Add peppers, 1/2 TBSP of salt and cook for 3 mins
  7. Add garlic, onion powder, garlic powder, pepper and remaining salt
  8. Reduce heat to low cook until onions are translucent
  9. Remove pan from heat
  10. Heat a griddle (or fry pan) on med heat
  11. Warm a tortilla until small blisters start to form then flip tortilla and repeat
  12. Assemble tacos in the following order:
    • tortilla
    • Montezuma Skirt Steak
    • onion, peppers
    • lime squeeze
    • salsa verde
These tacos were the reason why Cortez decided to conquer Mexico. Enjoy!
